HonestAI

About the Project

HonestAI is designed to bring transparency and credibility to users in an age of digital overload and confusion. Created for the Fall 2024 BMCC AI Innovation Challenge, this project utilizes AI/Machine Learning to help users analyze and verify web sources, promoting informed decisions in an era of digital information overload. Through machine learning and natural language processing, HonestAI provides users with tools to assess the credibility of content and to identify trustworthy information sources.

Next Steps

HonestAI has the potential to grow and serve broader audiences by scaling its technology and expanding its feature set. Click below to view the next steps for the project:

Develop more complex models to improve accuracy and refine the AI’s ability to detect misinformation patterns across various online platforms.

Create an API that allows other platforms, such as news aggregators and educational websites, to integrate HonestAI’s verification tools into their services.

Embed HonestAI within social media platforms to provide real-time credibility scoring on trending topics, helping users identify reliable information sources quickly.

Introduce tutorials, resources, and guides that help users understand how to evaluate information sources critically and use HonestAI to its fullest potential.
